Welcome to Clean Pour

Your Draft Line Cleaning Expert

At Clean Pour, we specialize in keeping your draft beer systems spotless and running smoothly. Whether you manage a bar, restaurant, brewery, or taproom, we ensure every pour is fresh, crisp, and clean. Our professional draft line cleaning services help maintain the quality and taste of your beverages while extending the life of your equipment. Trust Clean Pour to keep your lines clean and your customers coming back for more.

For your business, cleaner draft lines mean reduced product loss—some customers see an increased output of 8-10 pints per keg*—and improved beverage quality. This translates to happier customers, lower costs, and higher profits going right back into your pocket.

Our regular service includes caustic circulation cleaning, where circulation methods are used to ensure the most effective cleaning of your draft system and eliminate bacterial growth. We also perform hardware cleaning during every visit, removing and sanitizing faucets and sterilizing couplers—something static methods often fail to do.

Quarterly acid cleaning is another vital part of our maintenance plan. In addition to bi-weekly caustic cleaning, regular acid maintenance can extend the life of your system from just a few good years to well over a decade. During these quarterly visits, will perform:

  • Acid line cleaning, which removes off-flavors and beer stone buildup—major contributors to turbulence, debris, and excessive foaming.
  • Hardware deep-cleaning, including complete disassembly and sterilization of all faucets, along with coupler sterilization.
  • Beer and gas line jumper maintenance. Because line jumpers are porous, we recommend replacing them every 18 months to maintain system integrity and beverage quality.
